How to Play

Match 3+ gems in a line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to clear them. Cascades chain for bonus points. Every 10 clears levels you up and speeds the game.

Controls:

  • ← / → Move column left/right
  • ↑ Rotate gems in column
  • ↓ Soft drop (place immediately)
  • Space Hard drop (instant place)
  • P Pause game
  • M Mute/unmute audio

Scoring:

  • 3 gems = 300 points
  • 4 gems = 400 points
  • 5+ gems = 500+ points
  • Combos add +10% per chain

Game ends when gems stack to the top. Chase your high score!
