How to Play

Reorder the falling column and slide it into place. Match three or more identical gems in a line—horizontal, vertical, or diagonal—to clear them. Cascades can chain for bonus points. Levels increase every 10 clears and speed up the game.

  • Move: ⬅️ / ➡️ or on-screen arrows
  • Rotate: ⬆️ or ↻
  • Soft Drop: ⬇️
  • Hard Drop: Space / ⤓
  • Mute: Mute button
