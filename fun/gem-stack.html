How to Play

Reorder and drop the three-gem column to make lines of three or more matching colors. Lines clear and cascade; chain clears for big combos. The speed increases as you level up. Fill the stack to the top and it’s game over.

  • Mobile: use the on-screen buttons.
  • Desktop: ← → to move, ↑ to rotate, ↓ to soft drop, Space for hard drop, P to pause, M to mute.
