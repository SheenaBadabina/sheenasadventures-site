💡 Pro Tips

🎯

Plan Ahead

Look at the colors in your falling column and set up future matches

⛓️

Create Cascades

Position gems so clearing one match causes another - chain bonuses add up fast!

🔄

Use Rotation Wisely

Reorder your gems before placing - sometimes rotating saves a bad position

📐

Horizontal Mode

Toggle orientation (Space) to lay gems flat - great for filling gaps!

🏔️

Keep It Flat

Avoid tall stacks in the middle - spread gems out for more matching options

⚡

Speed Matters

Early levels are forgiving - use them to practice and build strategy
